What it is

Dripline is a pipeline with an internally built drip emitter, evenly spaced along the pipe to give a dedicated amount of water. Commonly used in Landscape and Effluent application HOOW DRIP PIPE MADE- After laminating the emitter to the extruded film, an exit hole is formed through the film to each emitter and the film is then wrapped and bead sealed to form a continuous flexible drip irrigation tube with the emitters spaced apart along the inside of the tube SIZE - The 1/2″ sizes of drip supply tubing are the most common sizes in use today, but there are also larger sizes produced and installed, although they are mainly used in commercial and agricultural installations.
